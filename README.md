# FOCUS HUBS

A simple field hub locator for Focus fiber work.

## What it does

- Import the Focus fiber hub Excel workbook.
- Search by OLT, hub number, road, development, cabinet, or notes.
- Open estimated hub locations in Google Maps.
- Save your exact GPS position when standing at a hub/cabinet.
- Keep recent hubs, favorites, and a simple route pad.
- Store imported hub data and verified GPS pins locally on your phone.

## Field workflow

1. Open the app.
2. Import the Focus hub workbook once.
3. Search a hub like `SSDF 3`.
4. Tap **Navigate** to open Google Maps.
5. When standing at the cabinet, tap **Save GPS**.
6. Next time, the app uses your saved coordinates instead of the estimated spreadsheet description.

## Notes

Verified GPS pins are stored in your browser's local storage on the phone/device you use. They are not uploaded anywhere yet.
